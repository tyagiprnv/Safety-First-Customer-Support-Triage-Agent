# Account Access and Security Policy

## Account Creation
- Accounts require a valid email address
- Password must be at least 12 characters
- Email verification required before full access
- One account per email address
- Business accounts can have multiple users

## Account Security

### Password Requirements
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include: uppercase, lowercase, number, special character
- Cannot reuse last 5 passwords
- Password reset available via email
- Passwords expire every 90 days (enterprise accounts only)

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- Optional for Basic and Professional plans
- Required for Enterprise plans
- Supported methods: authenticator apps, SMS (SMS as backup only)
- Recovery codes provided upon setup
- Contact support if you lose access to 2FA device

### Login Security
- Account locked after 5 failed login attempts
- 30-minute lockout period or email unlock link
- Login activity logged and visible in account settings
- Suspicious login attempts trigger email alerts
- Can review all active sessions and force logout

## Password Reset Process
If you've forgotten your password:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your account email address
3. Check email for reset link (valid for 1 hour)
4. Create new password meeting requirements
5. All active sessions logged out upon password change

If you don't receive the reset email:
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify you're using the correct email address
- Wait 5 minutes and request again
- Contact support if issues persist

## Account Access Issues

### "Account Locked" Message
Causes:
- Too many failed login attempts
- Suspicious activity detected
- Account flagged for security review
- Payment issues (see billing policy)

Solutions:
- Wait 30 minutes and try again
- Use email unlock link sent to your address
- Contact support for immediate assistance
- Update payment method if billing-related

### "Invalid Credentials" Message
Causes:
- Incorrect username or password
- Caps Lock enabled
- Password recently changed
- Account not yet verified

Solutions:
- Verify email address is correct
- Check Caps Lock and typing
- Use password reset if needed
- Check email for verification link

### Can't Access Account After Email Change
- Check both old and new email for verification
- Verification required before email change completes
- Login with old email until change is verified
- Contact support if verification email not received

## Login Troubleshooting

### Browser Issues
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try incognito/private browsing mode
- Disable browser extensions
- Update browser to latest version
- Try different browser

### "Session Expired" Message
- Normal after 7 days of inactivity
- Security measure to protect your account
- Simply log in again
- Can stay logged in longer by enabling "Remember Me"

### "Account Not Found"
- Verify email address spelling
- Check if you created account with social login
- Account may have been deleted (>90 days inactive)
- Try all email addresses you might have used

## Account Information Management

### Updating Email Address
1. Log into account
2. Navigate to Settings > Account
3. Enter new email address
4. Verify new email via link sent
5. Change completes after verification

Note: Cannot use email already associated with another account

### Updating Personal Information
- Name, company, and address can be updated anytime
- Changes do not require verification
- Billing address changes may affect tax calculation
- Some information required for compliance purposes

### Viewing Account Activity
- Access logs available in Settings > Security
- Shows login times, locations, and devices
- Download full activity log (30-day history)
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## Account Sharing and Transfer
- Do not share login credentials
- Add users through proper user management features
- Account transfer requires contacting support
- Business account ownership can be reassigned
- Personal accounts cannot be transferred

## Data Access and Privacy
- You own all data you upload
- Access your data anytime while account is active
- Export all data via Settings > Data Export
- Data deleted 90 days after account closure
- See Privacy Policy for detailed information

## Account Deletion
To permanently delete your account:
1. Export any data you want to keep
2. Navigate to Settings > Account > Delete Account
3. Confirm deletion request
4. Account marked for deletion
5. 30-day grace period to recover account
6. Permanent deletion after 30 days

Note: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone after grace period

---

**Support Contact**: If you cannot access your account, contact support with:
- Email address associated with account
- Approximate date of last login
- Any recent activity (payments, emails received, etc.)
- We may ask security questions to verify identity
