# Subscription Policy

## Subscription Plans
We offer three tiers of service to meet different customer needs:

### Basic Plan
- $29/month or $290/year (save 16%)
- Up to 5 users
- 10GB storage
- Email support (24-hour response time)
- Core features included

### Professional Plan
- $79/month or $790/year (save 16%)
- Up to 25 users
- 100GB storage
- Priority email support (12-hour response time)
- Advanced features included
- API access

### Enterprise Plan
- Custom pricing
- Unlimited users
- 1TB+ storage
- Phone and email support (4-hour response time)
- All features included
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom integrations

## Subscription Management

### Starting a Subscription
- Sign up through our website with a valid email address
- Choose your plan (monthly or annual billing)
- Enter payment information
- Receive immediate account access
- 14-day free trial available for new customers

### Changing Your Plan
You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time:

**Upgrading**:
- Takes effect immediately
- Prorated charge for the remainder of current billing period
- New features available instantly
- User and storage limits increase immediately

**Downgrading**:
- Takes effect at the end of current billing period
- No partial refunds for downgrades
- Data must fit within new plan limits
- Excess users will lose access at period end

### Canceling Your Subscription
- Cancellation can be initiated from your account settings
- Service continues until the end of paid period
- No refunds for partial months/years
- Data export available for 30 days after cancellation
- Account deletion occurs 90 days after cancellation unless requested sooner

## Free Trial Terms
- 14-day free trial for first-time customers
- Credit card required but not charged during trial
- Full access to chosen plan features
- Automatic conversion to paid subscription after trial
- Cancel anytime during trial period with no charge
- One free trial per customer

## Renewal and Auto-Billing
- Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each period
- You will receive a renewal reminder 7 days before billing
- Auto-renewal can be disabled in account settings
- If auto-renewal is disabled, service ends at period close
- Manual renewal option available through account dashboard

## Subscription Status

### Active
- All features accessible
- Billing is current
- Normal support access

### Past Due
- Payment failed but retries pending
- Service continues during retry period (7 days)
- Limited access to billing-related features
- Update payment method to restore full access

### Suspended
- All payment attempts failed
- Read-only access to data
- Cannot create or modify content
- Restore by updating payment method and settling balance

### Canceled
- Service ends at period close
- Data accessible for 30 days
- Can reactivate by resuming subscription
- All data deleted after 90 days

## User Seat Management
- User seats are included based on plan tier
- Additional seats available for $10/user/month
- Seat assignments can be changed anytime
- Unused seats do not roll over or credit future months
- Removing users does not reduce subscription cost until next renewal

## Storage Limits
- Storage calculated across all account data
- Includes files, documents, and databases
- Approaching limits trigger email warnings at 80% and 95%
- Exceeding limits may restrict new uploads
- Upgrade plan or delete data to restore functionality

## Educational and Nonprofit Discounts
- 25% discount available for verified educational institutions
- 20% discount for registered nonprofit organizations
- Verification required through third-party service
- Discount applied starting next billing cycle
- Contact support team to initiate verification

## Fair Use Policy
While we offer unlimited features on certain plans, all use must be reasonable:
- No excessive API calls (>1000 requests/minute sustained)
- No automated scraping or data mining
- No reselling of service
- Storage must be for legitimate business purposes
- Violation may result in account suspension
